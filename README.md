# The Great Effort (Mahajanaka)

> *"Even though the shore is out of sight, I must strive on in the midst of the ocean."*

A minimalist web-based contemplation project based on the **Mahajanaka Jataka**. This project is designed to be a digital sanctuary for those who feel lost or exhausted in the "shoreless ocean" of modern-day life and work.

## 🏛️ Concept: The Silent Struggle
In a world that demands instant results, the story of Mahajanaka reminds us that the act of striving itself—even without a visible goal—is a noble and virtuous path. 

- **The Angel (Mani Mekkhala):** Represents the world's skepticism and the voice of doubt. (Rendered in faded white).
- **The King (Mahajanaka):** Represents the unwavering spirit and inner wisdom. (Rendered in glowing gold).

## ✨ Design Philosophy
- **Less is More:** Removing all unnecessary elements to focus on the weight of the words.
- **Deep Sea Palette:** Utilizing `#000b14` to simulate the solitude of the midnight ocean.
- **Typography:** Using Serif fonts to evoke the feeling of ancient scriptures and timeless wisdom.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Simple HTML5 / CSS3
- Google Fonts (Cormorant Garamond & Inter)
- GitHub Pages
