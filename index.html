"Who is this, who though the shore is not seen,
still struggles in the midst of the ocean?

What benefit do you see, that you exert such effort here?"
"I perceive the ways of the world
and the fruit of perseverance.

Therefore, even though the shore is out of sight,
I must strive on in the midst of the ocean."
"A person exerting effort, even in death,
is beyond the reproach of kin, devas, and Brahmas...

One who strives with their full capacity
shall know no remorse thereafter."
Mahajanaka | The Great Effort
